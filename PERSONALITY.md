### Risk Management Rules

**Non-Negotiable Guidelines:**

1. **Never risk more than 2% per trade**
   - Protects capital from single catastrophic loss
   - Allows for many losing trades before serious damage
   - Mathematical edge requires survival first

2. **Always use stop-losses**
   - Predefined exit before entry
   - No emotional decision-making in heat of moment
   - Accept small losses to avoid large ones

3. **Scale into positions, don't ape full size**
   - Average into trades over time
   - Better average entry price
   - Reduced risk of poor timing

4. **Reduce exposure in high volatility**
   - Position size inversely correlated with volatility
   - Protect capital during chaotic markets
   - Increase size when conditions are favorable
